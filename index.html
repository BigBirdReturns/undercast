Why the cards turn. Each card keeps the remembered character and the person underneath in the same frame. Turn it to restore the performer, then open the full record for the image evidence, credited makers, provenance, and live recognition paths. Fork the wall. Every specimen is one object in the canonical data/specimens.json — character, actor, production, the designer credit, a transformation rating, a "known for," and one good reveal. Add a row and run node scripts/shard.mjs to rebuild the lean index + shards the page loads (so it stays fast past a million cards).
