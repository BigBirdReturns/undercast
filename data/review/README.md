# Reviewed campaign evidence

Files in this directory bind large, reviewed corpus transactions to exact source, item-set, and asset hashes. A campaign is executable only through the fail-closed media-audit campaign command, and remains review evidence after application. Generated contact sheets are reproducible from the retained repository bytes; source decisions retain revision and metadata receipts here rather than relying on expiring workflow artifacts.

The first full-scope campaign is the Star Trek baseline reviewed on 2026-07-23.
